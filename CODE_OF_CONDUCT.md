## 🌟 Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and leaders of the Cherry-101 community, pledge to make participation a **harassment-free experience for everyone**, regardless of:

- Age, body size, or physical appearance
- Disability or neurodivergence
- Ethnicity, nationality, or cultural background
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience or expertise
- Education
- Socioeconomic status
- Religion or personal beliefs
- Sexual orientation

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an **open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community**.

## ⚖️ Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers at **Cherry Computer Ltd.** are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They have the right and responsibility to **remove, edit, or reject** comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## 📣 Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening a confidential GitHub Issue or by contacting the project maintainers directly through GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion and care.

---

## 🔨 Enforcement Guidelines

| Violation Level | Consequence |
|:---:|:---|
| 🟡 **Minor** | Private written warning with clarity on the violation |
| 🟠 **Moderate** | Temporary restriction from community interaction |
| 🔴 **Severe** | Permanent ban from all project spaces |
